Top Hotels in Limpopo

Mabalingwe Nature Reserve

Mabalingwe Nature Reserve R516 Rooiberg Rd Thabazimbi Road, Bela-Bela

7Mabalingwe, is located 28 km west of Bela Bela, in the Limpopo province of South Africa, and is about 8 800 hectare in area. It is in an easily accessible, malaria-free area of the Transvaal bushveld, around an hour-and-a-half drive from Johannesburg. Mabalingwe means "Spots of the leopard.".

Blyde River Cabins

Off of the D240 Essex Road, Portion 20 Middlesex Farm, Hoedspruit

Blyde River Cabin Guesthouse is an upmarket luxury self-catering (catered on request) log cabin with an extensive private deck on the banks of the Blyde River (Limpopo Province, South Africa) situated on the fringe of a critically endangered azonal forest type known as Lowveld Riverine Forest

Bushriver Lodge

Oxford Farm, Hoedspruit, 13806

Self-catering, owner-operated, 4-star accommodation in luxury chalets on the banks of the Olifants River, close to the Kruger National Park and a mere 25 km from Hoedspruit.
