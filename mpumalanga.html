Mpumalanga

Top Attractions in Mpumalanga

Blyde River Canyon Nature Reserve

Blyde River Canyon Nature Reserve in Mpumalanga, South Africa forms part of the Greater Drakensberg escarpment and features on the scenic Panorama Route.

Lisbon Falls

308-ft. waterfall cascading over a craggy canyon wall in an expansive, rugged natural setting.

Mac Mac Falls

Twin waterfalls plunging 70 m., with a trail leading to a viewing platform & a series of pools.

Top Hotels in Mpumalanga

Riverview Inn

Riverview Inn in Malelane offers a 4-star hotel experience with a sun terrace, garden, outdoor swimming pool, and free WiFi. Guests enjoy a restaurant serving dinner and a comfortable outdoor seating area.

Walkersons Hotel & Spa

Located in Dullstroom, 10 miles from Dullstroom Railway Station, Walkersons Hotel & Spa provides accommodations with a fitness center, free private parking, a garden and a terrace.

Blyde Canyon, A Forever Resort

Boasting a swimming pool with views of the gorge, Blyde Canyon a Forever Resort offers self-catering rooms with BBQ facilities. The property provides views of the Three Rondavels, while a children's playground and tennis court is available to guests.
