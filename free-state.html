Top Attractions in Free State

Destiny Castle

Creating a real-life Medieval Castle in the Highlands of the Eastern Free State was a task of epic proportions. A 2-Part Mission undertaken with vision, patience and sensitivity, Castle construction was completed by the dedicated and unwavering Fogg family at the beginning of this century.

Clarens

Clarens is a small town situated in the foothills of the Maluti Mountains in the Free State province of South Africa and nicknamed the "Jewel of the Eastern Free State". It was established in 1912 and named after the town of Clarens in Switzerland where exiled Paul Kruger spent his last days.

Vaal Dam

The Vaal Dam is a large dam located on the Vaal River in South Africa, primarily serving as a crucial water reservoir for the Gauteng province and surrounding areas.

Top Hotels in Free State

Protea Hotel Clarens

This luxurious four-star retreat is nestled in the foothills of the Maluti Mountains, near the Golden Gate National Park and the Mountain Kingdom of Lesotho. Protea Hotel by Marriott Clarens exudes sheer style, luxury and design that blends in with the natural splendour of Clarens

Kiara Lodge

Set at the breathtakingly picturesque foot of the grand Maluti Mountains, Kiara Lodge borders the Golden Gate Highlands National Park and is a short drive from the quaint village of Clarens.

Thanda Tau

Thanda Tau is in essence, a blend of luxury and South African wildlife where you are able to relax and rejuvenate, and most importantly, to enjoy your time with friends and family.

Top Restaurants in Free State

De Oude Kraal

Marie Lombard — owner and chef of De Oude Kraal — has a 5th generational deep-rooted love for the Free State, and finds inspiration from fresh ingredients, using a wide variety of products from the farm. We offer elegant farm food and traditional South African food with a modern flair, serving farm breakfasts, high tea, lunches, afternoon tea, picnics and fine dining dinners.

New York Restaurant

This well-known and very popular restaurant was established in 1989. Since then New York Restaurant brought many friends and family together, treating their guests to a fine dine and exquisite cuisine.

Bella Casa Restaurant

Bella Casa Restaurant in Bloemfontein is a charming Italian eatery that exudes a warm and inviting trattoria-style ambiance. Established in 1989, this popular restaurant offers a delightful menu featuring authentic Italian cuisine, including wood-fired pizzas, homemade pastas, and an array of meat and seafood dishes..
