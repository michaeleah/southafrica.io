Top Attractions in North West

Aerial Cableway Hartbeespoort

A Swiss engineered and installed cableway elevates you to views of the beautiful Magaliesberg Mountains, Hartbeespoort Dam and surrounding areas, all while enjoying world-class recreational activities.

Sun City

Home to the Valley of Waves and sprawled along the border of the Pilanesberg National Park, Sun City Resort enjoys the fine distinction of being the only surf-and-safari destination within a two-hour drive of landlocked Johannesburg.

Little Paris

Little Paris Harties is in the Heart of Hartbeespoort where you will find a Little Eiffel Tower. Viewing Only Entry: R50 Free for Restaurant Customers.

Top Restaurants in North West

The Windmill Restaurant

Nestled in the picturesque Hartbeespoort, The Windmill Restaurant is a delightful family-friendly eatery housed within an iconic Dutch windmill. This charming spot is perfect for any meal of the day, offering a menu that features authentic Dutch cuisine alongside beloved South African dishes.

Die Ou Pastorie

Nestled on the outskirts of the Cradle of Humankind, Die Ou Pastorie is a charming guesthouse that offers a delightful escape into nature.

Die Plaas se Gat Restaurant

Die Plaas se Gat Restaurant is a casual, farm-like venue with rustic charm, offering good food and craft beer. It's a great place to chill with friends, enjoy hearty food, and live music on most weekends. The outdoor seating is close to the kiddies play area. The menu includes Boere Kospap, podjiekos, steak, and more at very reasonable prices.
