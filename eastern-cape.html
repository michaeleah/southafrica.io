Eastern Cape

Top Attractions in Eastern Cape

Valley of Desolation

The road to the top of the valley was tarred in 1978 and today provides tourists with easy access to the viewpoints with their panoramic views of the landscape.

Sardinia Bay Beach

Sardinia Bay Beach, affectionately known as "Sards" by locals, is a beautiful, secluded beach located near Port Elizabeth in the Eastern Cape of South Africa. It's known for its long stretches of white sand, backed by impressive sand dunes, and clear blue waters.

Otter Trail

The Otter Trail is a renowned 5-day, 4-night hiking trail located in the Tsitsikamma section of the Garden Route National Park in South Africa, known for its stunning coastal scenery and challenging terrain.

Top Hotels in Eastern Cape

Beach House Jeffreys Bay

Enjoy fantastic sea views, sunrises over the ocean and dolphins in the bay as they swim by – all from the comfort of your bed. Jeffreys Bay Beach House has modern rooms with en-suite bathrooms and private decks overlooking the ocean and we also offer free WiFi, selected DSTV channels, tea and coffee facilities in all rooms as well as much much more.

Paxton Hotel

Close to the airport and marina, the Paxton Hotel features 80 spacious guest accommodations. Marvel at views of the harbor from the comfort of your balcony. If you wish, you can order in room service and check your email via free wired internet.

Blue Lagoon Hotel and Conference Centre

Nestled in indigenous, sub-tropical foliage and where the Nahoon River meets the warm Indian Ocean, the Blue Lagoon offers tranquility and seclusion just outside East London. It features an outdoor pool, a separate children’s pool, and a BBQ area.

Top Restaurants in Eastern Cape

Hello You Restaurant

Welcome to HELLO YOU, a culinary haven where flavour meets creativity, and every dish tells a story. Nestled in the heart of Graaff Reinet, HELLO YOU is not just a restaurant; it's a vibrant celebration of culinary artistry and a warm invitation to savour the extraordinary.

Kitchen Windows Beach Restaurant

Kitchen Windows Beach Restaurant is situated on the beach to the left of the famous surf spot, 'Kitchen Windows'. Great seafood, steak, tapas, pizza etc. for the whole family.

Tasty Table

Tasty Table Cafe is a Family run Cafe with an incredible view of the Blue Flag, Main Beach of Jeffreys Bay, also known as Dolphin beach .... for good reason. We are known for our Speciality Coffees, Burgers, Seafood and Freshly baked Treats.
